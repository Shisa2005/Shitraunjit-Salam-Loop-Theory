Core Theories at a Glance

Time as a Loop, Not a Line

Time does not move forward — it cycles. Every moment exists in a loop. What feels like progression is re-entry.

Simultaneity of Birth and Death

Birth and death are not separate events but part of the same loop structure. Life exists as a simultaneous architecture of moments.

The Brain as a 4D Parasite

The brain functions as a time-regulating entity, selectively sharing awareness and memory based on what the 3D body can process.

Dreams as Dimensional Breaches

Dreams may represent access points into alternate loops or timelines when the brain’s 4D gateway briefly opens.

Mental Anomalies as Oversharing

Conditions such as schizophrenia, autism, or DID may involve excess dimensional information interacting with a 3D biological system.
